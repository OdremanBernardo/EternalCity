The Sovereign View

Il Buco della Serratura

A simple green door on Aventine Hill holds a secret: look through the keyhole to see three sovereign nations perfectly aligned in a single glance.

Memento Mori

Capuchin Crypt

An evocative encounter with the friars of the past. Chapels decorated with the bones of 4,000 monks—a hauntingly beautiful lesson in history and faith.

Hydraulic Alchemy

Pincio Water Clock

Hidden in the heart of Villa Borghese, this 19th-century engineering marvel tells time using only the steady, rhythmic flow of water.

The Epic Noon

Belvedere del Gianicolo

The most cinematic balcony in Rome. Every day at noon, a cannon fire echoes across the valley, marking time above the city's golden rooftops.
